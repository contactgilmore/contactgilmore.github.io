About Me

I build and support systems that don’t fall over when things get weird—and I’ve been doing it long enough to know that weird is the norm. My background spans cloud engineering, DevOps, and site reliability, but honestly, what keeps me going is the puzzle-solving. I like digging into problems, fixing what’s broken, and finding better ways to do things—even if it means rewriting the map.

Before I was an SRE, I led teams in the retail world, which taught me how to stay calm under pressure, connect with people, and actually listen—skills that ended up being just as valuable as learning Kubernetes. That blend of human and technical perspective has shaped how I work today: clear priorities, good communication, and steady execution (especially when the alerts won’t stop).

Over the years, I’ve helped teams roll out global onboarding programs, simplify chaos with solid documentation, and get to the root of gnarly incidents. But the work I’m proudest of is the kind that makes life easier for the folks around me—whether that’s a teammate, a customer, or the next person on-call at 2am.

Thanks for stopping by.
